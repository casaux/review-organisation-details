Aviation reference number

To access myCASA services you will need an aviation reference number (ARN) and proof of identification to verify your identity.

This will require one of the following documents, so please have one of these ready before continuing:

  • Australian passport
  • Australian birth certificate
  • Australian citizenship certificate
  • Foreign passport
  • ImmiCard
