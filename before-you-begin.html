Organisation ARN application

Back

An organisation ARN is required for any organisation to interact with CASA. The following types of organisations can apply for an organisation ARN online and receive one generally within a few minutes:

  • companies (government, public and private)
  • statutory authorities (Commonwealth, state, local)
  • government entities; (departments, agencies, schools)
  • incorporated companies
  • other incorporated association and/or entities
  • sole traders
  • co-operatives

For any other type of organisation, please refer to CASA guidance on applying for organisation ARNs.

By applying for an organisation ARN you will be recorded as the authorised representative for this organisation and you must have the appropriate authorisation to make this application.
